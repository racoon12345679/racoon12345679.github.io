Three is an archive of my 3 years in Melbourne.

On October the 4th, 2014, I stepped on the plane to Melbourne and started my life abroad.
The same morning, my grandfather's funeral was held.
It was two months before that we found out he had lung cancer.
To me, grandpa was quiet, strict yet kind. He was "the nerd" in our family. complete opposite to me back then, I didn't realize the last part until I saw him lying there, lifelessly.
I kept remembering how he promised me he would definitely come to the airport and see me off.
There was not enough time for me to mourne, I came to this conclusion as the plane was taking off.
