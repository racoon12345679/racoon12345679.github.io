YUNYI. DESIGN
The aim of this project is to explore the connection amongst graphic design, art, and film through an adaptable and evolving visual identity for ACMI Melbourne Cinematheque.

The Melbourne Cinémathèque is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run film society. An A1 program, an A2 poster series and an animated poster were designed as an application for the 2018 Melbourne Cinémathèque identity.
